蛋挞

Xiaolin Zhang

甜品

Desert consists of an outer pastry crust filled with egg custard

Servings

6 Servings

Prep time

30 Minutes

Cooking Time

15 Minutes

Calories

80 Kcal

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 60g milk
  • 70g heavy cream
  • 20g sugar

Directions

  1. Defrost the pastry crust(蛋挞皮) in the room for 20mins
  2. Mix the ingredients and fill the crust
  3. Preheat the oven to 450(F) degrees
  4. Bake for 15mins
  5. Rest to room temperature
