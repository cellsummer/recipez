Busque Cheesecake

Xiaolin Zhang

甜品

Basque cheesecake is Light and caramelized on the top with a rich, gooey interior.

Servings

4 Servings

Prep time

30 Minutes

Cooking Time

50 Minutes

Calories

200 Kcal

Ingredients

  • 600g cream cheese
  • 225g sugar
  • A pinch of salt
  • 4 eggs
  • 300g heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 40g flour

Directions

  1. Put all ingredients in a mixer and mix on medium speed until fully combined and smooth
  2. Set on a baking sheet inside a cake pan.
  3. Preheat the oven to 200(F) degrees
  4. Bake for 50 minutes
  5. Let the cheesecake cool in the pan at room temperature for 3 hours
